# MINERD — Matemática Nivel Primario

> **Source:** Adecuación Curricular Nivel Primario — Área de Matemática (MINERD 2023)
> **Relevance:** Primary curriculum alignment for EducaT Phase 1 (Grades 1–3 MVP)
> **Ref:** https://ministeriodeeducacion.gob.do/sobre-nosotros/areas-institucionales/direccion-general-de-curriculo/diseno-curricular

---

## Definición del Área

Math curriculum grounded in constant transformation and development — values both process and results. Primary level focuses math as: tool for life + connection to other disciplines. First Cycle emphasizes **initial numeric literacy** integrated with reading/writing. Problem solving is central strategy across all levels and cycles.

**Perfil del egresado:** resolves problems in diverse contexts, translates mathematical language, makes conjectures, explores and discovers, builds new knowledge autonomously.

---

## Propósito (Nivel Primario)

Develop mathematical thinking + build foundational knowledge base for: interpreting/understanding the environment, successful transition to Secondary level, continuation of personal formation and mathematical capacities.

---

## Campos del Conocimiento

Three IMU vertientes: Matemática pura · Matemática aplicada · Matemática educativa

External connections: Física, Química, Ingenierías, Economía, Ciencias Médicas, Artes, Historia, Antropología, Sociología, Biología, Informática.

## Contextualización — Primer Ciclo

**Four knowledge blocks:** Numeración · Geometría · Medición · Estadística

**Cycle purposes:**
- Incorporate mathematical expressions into language for precise/rigorous communication
- Interpret mathematical models and patterns at level
- Solve math problems coherently and creatively with manipulative and digital resources
- Use data collection techniques for judgements on facts and situations
- Critically analyze problems on environment, climate change, citizenship, and community projects using mathematical models autonomously

## Competencias Fundamentales — Primer Ciclo

### Comunicativa
**Cycle competency:** Communicates orally and in writing using numerical language and diverse representations, to establish relationships between concepts and context.

| Grade | Specific Competency |
|---|---|
| 1ro | Relates physical materials, images, diagrams to mathematical ideas to solve elementary problems |
| 2do | Explains orally and in writing ideas on concepts/situations with mathematical content, linking daily language with mathematical symbols |
| 3ro | Represents — reading, writing, interpreting, discussing — comprehension of math problem statements, concepts, and operation properties |

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Expression of mathematical ideas in problem solving from daily environment
- Relating everyday language to mathematical symbols
- Reading, writing, translating mathematical expressions with increasing precision and rigor

---

### Pensamiento Lógico, Creativo y Crítico + Resolución de Problemas + Científica y Tecnológica
**Cycle competency:** Uses adequate strategies and procedures for addressing/solving mathematical problem situations from daily reality and other disciplines; perceives with logical validity patterns and structures in symbolic objects and daily context situations; integrates digital technologies actively and critically.

| Grade | Specific Competency |
|---|---|
| 1ro | Solves math problems coherently with manipulative and digital resources; identifies patterns, structures, regularities inductively |
| 2do | Uses problem-solving approach to understand math content, establishing and investigating conjectures to justify arguments and proofs |
| 3ro | Uses systematic problem-solving strategies, verifying/interpreting results vs. original situation; justifies own reasoning processes inductively using concrete resources and digital technologies |

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Logical reasoning facing math environment problems
- Use of different mathematical ideas for problem solving
- Application of problem-solving approach to investigate mathematical ideas
- Formulation of conjectures to justify arguments
- Recognition of concrete resources and technological tools
- Application of strategies inside and outside mathematics
- Justification of own reasoning about demonstrations/algorithms/results

## Metodología y Evaluación

**Key strategies (both cycles):** Socratic dialogue · Problem-based learning · Case study · Project-based learning · Recovery of prior experiences · Expository of accumulated knowledge · Discovery and inquiry · Teacher/student insertion in environment · Socialization centered in group activities

**Problem solving note:** NOT transferring data, solving mechanical exercises, or using formulas to substitute data. Level of analysis according to conceptual base — students make inferences and organize ideas to contribute to problem solution.

**Problem-solving steps (recommended):**
1. Read problem carefully to understand it
2. Make a scheme or design of the presented situation
3. Design a plan/strategy for resolution
4. Execute steps proposed in the plan
5. Look back to contrast with what the problem asks
6. Communicate the generated solution autonomously and responsibly

**Evaluation strategies:** Observation/anecdotal record · Concept maps · Portfolios · Reflective class diary · Debates · Common presentation · Oral exchanges · Interviews · Problem solving

## Mallas Curriculares — Detalle por Grado

### 1er. Grado — Primer Ciclo

**Competencias específicas:**
- Comunicativa: Relates physical materials, images, diagrams to mathematical ideas to solve elementary problems in daily environment
- Pensamiento Lógico/Resolución/Tecnológica: Solves problems coherently with manipulative and digital resources; identifies patterns/structures/regularities inductively
- Ética/Personal/Ambiental: Values coexistence norms and teamwork, respects classmates' ideas

#### Contenidos

**Numeración:**
- Natural numbers sequence minimum to 99
- Number patterns
- Ordinal numbers to tenth (10th)
- Place value: units and tens
- Addition of natural numbers minimum to 99
- Subtraction of natural numbers minimum to 99
- Symbols: =, <, >

**Procedimientos:**
- Counting numbers under 99 by different criteria
- Use of Abacus, Multibase, and color chips for place value representation minimum to 99
- Counting by 2s, 3s, 5s, 10s
- Use of ordinal numbers to 10th
- Composition and decomposition of numbers minimum to 99
- Problem solving using addition and subtraction
- Use of =, <, > to compare numbers under 99
- Project: role-play with bills and coins to 100 pesos

**Geometría:**
- Geometric solids: cube, sphere, cone, cylinder
- Rectangular prism and rectangular pyramid
- Flat figures: circle, triangle, square, rectangle
- Patterns and mosaics with geometric figures
- Lines: straight, open curves, closed curves, mixed

**Procedimientos geometría:**
- Comparison and classification of geometric solids by common characteristics
- Problem solving with rectangular prism and pyramid
- Identification and relation of shapes in school/family environment with geometric solids and flat figures
- Identification of patterns and mosaics with geometric figures
- Tracing of straight and curved lines, closed and mixed
- Use of concrete and technological resources (interactive)

**Medición:**
- Lengths and measurement
- Arbitrary units of length
- Length estimation
- Centimeter and inch intuitively

**Procedimientos medición:**
- Identification and drawing of objects using materials approximately 1 cm or 1 inch
- Estimation of lengths of daily objects using centimeter and inch
- Use of simple computer applications to measure and estimate length
- Exploration and intuitive comparison of weight of different objects

**Estadística:**
- Collection and organization of non-grouped data
- Tally tables
- Pictograms

**Procedimientos estadística:**
- Collection, organization, classification of non-grouped data
- Elaboration of tally tables given a series of distinct objects, marking as each is counted
- Elaboration and interpretation of pictograms with objects from environment: toys, pencils, foods
- Reading/interpretation of pictograms and simple data tables using technological tools

**Indicadores de Logro:**
- Demonstrates understanding of decimal numbering system relating counting, grouping, and place value
- Constructs meaning of addition and subtraction of natural numbers to 99 by modeling and discussing problem situations
- Expresses orally and in writing processes in problem solving for geometry and measurement
- Uses logical thinking to organize and relate diverse information from daily life and problem solving
- Describes, elaborates, draws, and classifies flat geometric figures and geometric solids
- Collects, organizes, describes data and constructs, reads, and interprets data in tally tables and pictograms
- Recognizes and describes diverse numerical and geometric patterns
- Integrates manipulative and digital resources to support understanding of mathematical ideas and problem solving
- Works collaboratively respecting different ideas and points of view in classroom mathematical work

- Pensamiento Lógico/Resolución/Tecnológica: Uses estimation and mathematical measurement to solve problems identified in community
- Ética/Personal/Ambiental: Respects others' points of view and arguments about situations involving mathematical knowledge in social/environmental context

#### Contenidos

**Numeración:**
- Natural numbers sequence minimum to 999

- Symbols: >, <, =

**Geometría:**
- Flat figures: circle, triangle, square, rectangle
- Similar and congruent figures
- Symmetry: symmetric figures with 1 or 2 lines of symmetry
- Lines of symmetry
- Geometric patterns
- Geometric solids: cube, triangular and rectangular prism, triangular pyramid, sphere, cone, cylinder
- Elements of geometric solids and flat figures in faces, position and spatial sense, cardinal points, displacements and routes

**Procedimientos geometría:**
- Identification of flat figures in daily life situations
- Identification of characteristics of similar and congruent figures
- Use of geometric figure models to relate to school/family environment
- Discovery of utility of congruent figures in daily situations
- Exploration of symmetries in: body, objects, geometric figures
- Construction of symmetric figures and location of symmetry line
- Identification of geometric solids in daily life situations
- Identification of parts of geometric solids in environment objects
- Problem solving recognizing flat figures in solid faces
- Location of points from spatial sense, cardinal points, displacements and routes

**Medición:**
- Arbitrary or conventional unit of measure
- Lengths: arbitrary units, meter, decimeter, centimeter
- Length estimation: rulers and measuring tape
- Capacity measures: arbitrary units, cup, pint, liter, gallon; estimation
- Time, clock and calendar
- Weight and money: coins and bills to 1,000 pesos

**Procedimientos medición:**
- Use of instruments (manipulative and digital) in diverse activities
- Use of body parts and environment materials as arbitrary units
- Measurement and estimation of capacity
- Time unit conversions: year→month, year→days, days→hours, hours→minutes, minutes→seconds
- Estimation of lengths in meters, decimeters, centimeters using ruler and measuring tape
- Project: role-play with bills and coins to 1,000 pesos

**Estadística:**
- Collection and organization of data in tables
- Pictograms
- Bar graphs

**Procedimientos estadística:**
- Collection of data on toy preferences, colors, TV programs, baseball teams, subjects in classroom
- Problem solving with collection, organization, tabulation, and interpretation of non-grouped data in tables, pictograms, bars
- Problem solving on organization/interpretation using computer and tech tools

**Indicadores de Logro:**
- Justifies from mathematical ideas processes in sequence and fundamental operations with natural numbers under 999; applies in diverse daily situations implying place value to hundreds
- Expresses correctly solutions to situations implying ordering and comparing natural numbers under 999
- Communicates from everyday language and mathematical symbols processes and regularities associated with patterns on number line with natural numbers under 999
- Formulates conjectures in logical reasoning of processes in classification of flat figures and geometric solids, links to school/family context
- Logically proposes solutions to spatial location/sense situations through object localization and routes, integrating cardinal points and displacements
- Applies diverse problem-solving strategies requiring measurement (arbitrary/conventional) of object lengths; applies in situations requiring estimation of lengths, capacity, time, money under 1,000 pesos
- Employs creativity to solve community problems related to collection/analysis of non-grouped statistical data to represent in pictograms and bar graphs
- Recognizes and uses manipulative and digital resources in situations linked to mathematics; links to modeling of mathematical situations in geometry, measurement, and non-grouped statistics
- Carries out projects in school context on diverse topics favoring environment, using knowledge of numeracy, geometry, measurement, statistics, respecting classmates' efforts and decisions

### 3er. Grado — Primer Ciclo

**Competencias específicas:**
- Comunicativa: Represents — reading, writing, interpreting, discussing — comprehension of math problem statements and operation concepts/properties to establish relations and context
- Pensamiento Lógico/Resolución/Tecnológica: Uses systematic problem-solving strategies, verifying/interpreting results; justifies own reasoning inductively using concrete and digital resources
- Ética/Personal/Ambiental: Interprets possible solutions to environmental situations from mathematical knowledge

#### Contenidos

**Numeración:**
- Natural numbers sequence minimum to 99,999
- Place value: units, tens, hundreds, thousands unit, ten-thousands
- Even and odd numbers
- Number line
- Signs: =, <, >, ≠ for comparison
- Ordinal numbers to thirtieth (30th)
- Mental calculation, addition properties
- Subtraction, subtraction proof, sum/difference estimation
- Multiplication and division as repeated addition/subtraction
- Multiplication properties
- Numerical patterns on number line
- Common fractions: proper and improper
- Unit fraction

**Procedimientos numeración:**
- Understanding numerical sequence minimum to 99,999
- Composition and decomposition from place value minimum to 99,999
- Use of =, <, >, ≠
- Problem solving with even/odd numbers on number line
- Ordering objects using ordinals to 30th
- Problem solving with natural numbers and exact fractions
- Problem solving with mental calculation, estimation, addition/subtraction properties
- Addition/subtraction operations with natural numbers not exceeding 99,999
- Multiplication: 1 digit × 2 digits, 1 digit × 3 digits, 2 digits × 2 digits, 2 digits × 3 digits not exceeding 99,999
- Division: 1 digit ÷ 1 digit, 3 digits ÷ 1 digit, 2 digits ÷ 2 digits, 3 digits ÷ 2 digits not exceeding 99,999
- Identification of numerical patterns on number line
- Classification of common fractions: proper, improper, unit fraction
- Identification of common fractions in school/community situations

**Geometría:**
- Intuitive concept of segment and ray
- Geometric solids: cube, rectangular prism, pyramid
- Polygons: triangles, quadrilaterals, pentagons, others
- Polygon elements: sides, vertices
- Angles: intuitive concept, right angles, acute angles, obtuse angles
- Congruence, congruent figures, congruent segments
- Translation, rotation, reflection
- Symmetry: symmetric figures, lines of symmetry
- Geometric patterns and tessellation
- Plane, points in plane, figures in plane, cardinal points, routes, sketches

**Procedimientos geometría:**
- Identification of pattern elements in nature phenomena
- Identification of polygon elements
- Application of intuitive segment/ray concept in daily/classroom situations
- Identification and construction of geometric solids
- Construction of polygons with different attributes using geoboard, identifying their elements
- Identification of angles in geometric figures and environment objects
- Discovery that congruent figures have same shape and size
- Application of translation, rotation, reflection in diverse situations
- Projects with: geometric patterns and tessellation, plane, points/figures in plane, cardinal points, routes, sketches, cube, rectangular prism, pyramid, tessellation
- Use of computer applications to explore angles
- Discovery of lines of symmetry in elements (leaves, butterflies, human figures, animal figures) by folding in different directions
- Use of computer applications to model locations and displacements in plane

**Medición:**
- Units: centimeter, decimeter, meter
- Units: inch, foot, yard
- Area and perimeter: measurement and estimation
- Perimeters of triangles and quadrilaterals
- Geoboard for measuring perimeter
- Arbitrary area units, square as area unit, area estimation, square centimeter
- Sum and subtraction of lengths
- Capacity measures, weight, volume
- Money: coins and bills to RD$100,000
- Capacity: cup, liter, half liter, equivalence between liter and cup
- Weight: unit, mass, pound, ounce, kilogram
- Time: clock, chronometer, hour, minute, second, calendar

**Procedimientos medición:**
- Problem solving with lengths in centimeters, inches, units
- Estimation and measurement of capacities of diverse containers using cup and liter
- Problem solving: cup, liter, half-liter, equivalence liter/cup, weight, mass, pound, ounce, kilogram
- Problem solving related to body weight
- Problem solving with time, clock, chronometer, hour, minute, second, calendar
- Use of tech tools for time measurement and event organization
- Problem solving with money: peso (RD$), national coin/bill, budget
- Project: role-play with bills and coins to RD$100,000
- Problem solving with area units, square as area unit, area estimation, square centimeter
- Perimeter problems of triangles and quadrilaterals

**Estadística:**
- Collection, organization, classification, registration in table, analysis of non-grouped data
- Tally tables
- Pictograms
- Bar graphs and line graphs

**Procedimientos estadística:**
- Classification and organization of data by defined criteria
- Problem solving related to collection, organization, analysis, interpretation of non-grouped data in tally tables, pictograms, bar graphs
- Use of tech tools to elaborate and represent non-grouped data in bar graphs and pictograms

**Indicadores de Logro:**
- Expresses from reading, writing, translation, mathematical ideas on topics of school context related to even/odd number sequences (to 99,999) on number line and connection with sense of numerical patterns
- Justifies from correct criteria, mathematical ideas implying place value of natural numbers to hundred-thousands, making comparisons using <, >, = associated with environment situations using ordinals to 30th
- Correctly interprets diverse situations from reading, writing, operating unit fractions and their types; links with other scenarios from mental calculation in properties and operations with natural numbers to 99,999
- Justifies from logical thinking, mathematical ideas related to intuitive concept of segment and ray in diverse situations associated with polygon forms and involved angles in community context scenarios
- Uses logical reasoning to interpret processes and represent figures in plane from mobilization of knowledge associated with congruence, symmetry, geometric bodies and patterns in architectural designs and natural/community context
- Applies appropriate problem-solving strategies from environment requiring arbitrary/conventional measurement of object lengths (meter, centimeter, decimeter); applies in school/family context for estimation and calculation of: lengths, perimeter, area, capacity, time, money under 100,000 pesos
- Solves problems from diverse techniques in community context, related to collection/analysis of non-grouped statistical data for representation/interpretation in bar and line graphs
- Employs creative and concrete/digital technological resources knowledge about school environment cases implying mathematical modeling on: numeracy (natural numbers under 99,999), geometry (segment/ray, polygons, congruence, angles, geometric bodies and patterns), measurement (lengths, perimeter, area, capacity, time, money), non-grouped statistics
- Analyzes family/community environment situations involving environment care knowledge, based on experiences with natural numbers under 99,999 and its connection with scenarios in geometry, measurement, non-grouped statistics
